Word Games!

Here is the classic word jumble game, where you, the user, will be presented with a random scrambled word. You will then have either 20/30/40 seconds to try and guess the correct word, depending on the level difficulty you choose. If you fail to unscramble the word in the time given, the game is over. You will get 10 points for each word you successfully unscramble. For every 10 words you get right, you will move on to the next level.
